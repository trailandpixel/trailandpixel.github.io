Why Trail & Pixel?

Curiosity has always shaped the way I experience the world.

Long before I owned a backpack or a camera, it led me to ask questions, to look beyond what I already knew, and to enjoy the journey of discovering rather than the certainty of arriving.

Travel eventually became the richest expression of that curiosity.

Not because it offered answers, but because every place, every person, and every unexpected encounter opened the door to new questions.

Trail & Pixel was born from the desire to preserve those moments before memory slowly reshapes them, and to share them with others.

Because knowledge grows when it is shared.
