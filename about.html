Trail & Pixel

The story behind every story.

Every story begins with a question.

Every journey is a quest for answers.

More often than not, we return carrying even more questions than the ones we left with.

Some journeys change where we are.

The journeys that stay with us change who we become.

Why Trail & Pixel?

Curiosity has always shaped the way I experience the world. Long before I owned a backpack or a camera, it led me to ask questions, look beyond what I already knew, and enjoy the journey of discovering more than the certainty of arriving.

Travel eventually became the richest expression of that curiosity — not because it offered answers, but because every place, every person, and every unexpected encounter opened the door to new questions.

Trail & Pixel was born from the desire to preserve those moments before memory slowly reshapes them, and to share them with others.

Because what is knowledge, if not shared?

Before there was Trail & Pixel...

I grew up in La Spezia, a maritime city in eastern Liguria, where the sea meets the Apennine mountains. Looking back, I realise that landscape quietly shaped me long before I understood how much it would influence the person I would become.

The sea led me to sailing. The mountains led me to hiking. And the horizon constantly made me wonder what existed beyond it.

Curiosity soon found another home in mathematics and later in physics. I loved asking questions, searching for patterns, and discovering the beauty hidden behind seemingly complex ideas. Physics became more than a subject — it became part of my identity and the way I learned to look at the world.

That same curiosity eventually carried me beyond the classroom. University took me away from home, and an Erasmus year in Munich changed the way I travelled forever. It was there that I discovered the joy of walking for hours without a destination, simply to explore, observe, and understand a place one street at a time.

Backpacking naturally followed. Then photography. Not as a way of collecting beautiful images, but as a way of preserving memories, paying attention, and remembering the emotions surrounding a single moment.

Today, curiosity still guides both my professional life as a research engineer and my personal life as a traveller. Trail & Pixel is simply where those two worlds meet — a place to collect stories, photographs, reflections, and the questions that every journey leaves behind.

What you'll find here

This is a place where I collect memories before they slowly fade and where photographs regain the emotions that surrounded them.

But if these stories can also inspire someone else to explore, ask questions, or simply become curious about the world around them, then they have already achieved more than I ever hoped.

You don't have to follow my footsteps.

I hope these stories inspire you to create your own journey.
