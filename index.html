Trail & Pixel

Journeys through landscapes and light

I’m a full-time engineer, traveling whenever I can. Somewhere along the way, I picked up a camera and started paying more attention — to light, to places, and to the small details that usually go unnoticed.

Trail & Pixel is my journal of trails walked and pixels captured. A personal record of journeys through mountains, cities, sailing, and everything in between. It’s a collection of photographs, memories, thoughts, and emotions gathered along the way.
